Open your project in WebStorm.

Go to the "Run" menu at the top, then select "Edit Configurations."

Click the "+" button and choose "Shell Script."

In the "Name" field, give your configuration a name

In the "Script" field, enter the full path to your script, including the script name, such as C:\Users\Click\Documents\angular\code\pwa\run-app.bat

Set the "Working directory" to the directory where your Angular project is located, such as C:/Users/Click/Documents/angular/code/pwa
